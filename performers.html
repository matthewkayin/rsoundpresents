Meet the Performers

Of R Sound Presents Night 49

Kenon Brinkley Kenon “KJ” Brinkley doesn’t know what he wants in his bio. Hell, he rarely knows what he’s doing on the mic, but he’s happy to dabble in anything from hosting, to singing, to rapping, to poetry, the tenor sax, or whatever he can get his hands on.
Kelsey Rawcliffe Kelsey is 21 years old, and a junior at Wichita State University in pursuit of a nursing degree. Kelsey solely performs poetry at R Sound shows but explores the visual arts through painting, drawing, and ceramics.
Cara La Gore Cara La Gore is a 21 year old college graduate and has been writing poetry for 5 years now. She has performed at rallies and marches in the past and says her work focuses on how her experiences and journeys have shaped her outlook on life.
Anthony Harvey Anthony Harvey is a local singer songwriter born and raised here in Wichita. He is also the lead guitarist in the local trio “Olympia”. Harvey began singing and playing at age 6 and hasn’t stopped since. He hopes to make a career out of music some day.
Insub Kim Hailing from Daegu, South Korea, Insub Kim says his life would be boring without music. Insub makes it clear that he is only a hobbiest, and not a professional. Despite this, Insub believes that his passion for music is heard in his work.
Jaiden Carter Jaiden Carter performs slam and spoken word poetry and has been for 3 years. Jaiden has also had a few small tours along the East coast.
Ellie Green Ellie Green is a return perfomer at R Sound Presents. She sings and plays acoustic guitar. Ellie describes herself as an aspiring musician.
Bri Murdock Bri Murdock is an acoustic singer-songwriter. A self-taught guitarist, Bri discovered she had a singing voice at 17 and has gone on to perform at gigs such as R Sound for the past 3 to 4 years.

Sage Judd Sage began playing piano when he was little and says he started to really focus on piano once he got into college. Sage has been performing professionally as a jazz pianist for 3 years, although his real passion is writing original music.
Grace Buckingham A Wichita native with a deep passion for travel, Grace picked up the ukulele as a way to continue playing music during a cross-continental adventure she embarked on when she was 19. Playing the ukulele has been a hobby of hers ever since.
Victoria Campbell Tori is a Senior at Wichita State whose passion for music formed as she participated in musicals at a young age. While Tori has been singing and playing guitar as a worship leader for 8 years, she is now looking to expand her performance in Wichita.
Alte' Alte' is a musician/rapper/singer who has been independently writing music for 7 years. He says music is a big part of his life and that he aims to inspire and uplift with his art.
William Bloom William has been doing the singer-songwriter thing around Wichita for about 7 years. He is also a dedicated instructor for young music students. William usually plays guitar, but you may find him playing keys or with his newly assembled band.
Marrice Anthony Marrice is a rapper from Wichita who has been performing for the better part of 12 years. An avid songwriter, Marrice says his work is as vulnerable as he can make it, and deals with themes like love, death, and human relationships.
